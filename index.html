About Me

My background in the Marine Corps taught me how to lead, collaborate under pressure, and execute with precision—qualities that now drive my approach to technical learning and professional growth. I’m motivated by challenges, eager to master new tools and technologies, and committed to contributing meaningfully to any team I join.
